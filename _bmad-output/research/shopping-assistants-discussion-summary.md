## üéì Key Market Insights

**1. Visual Search is Proven**
- Documented 35% adoption increase at Myntra
- Higher conversion rates vs. text search
- Especially critical for fashion, furniture, home d√©cor

**2. Personalization is Table Stakes**
- Generic recommendations no longer sufficient
- Customers expect individual preference learning
- Multi-channel consistency required

**3. AI + Human = Better Results**
- Pure AI works well for simple products
- Complex items (groceries, customizations) benefit from human judgment
- Hybrid models showing strongest customer satisfaction

**4. Voice is Growing**
- Alexa, Google Home, smart speakers mainstream
- Voice commerce expected capability, not novelty
- Hands-free shopping during meal prep, commuting common use case

**5. Integration > Standalone**
- Solutions embedded in platforms winning
- API-first approach critical for adoption
- Omnichannel consistency essential
